What to Expect at Your First Visit

Your initial consultation at Cosmetic Dental Cape Town is designed to be informative, comfortable, and pressure-free. Here's what you can expect:

1. Warm Welcome

Our friendly reception team will greet you and help you complete any necessary paperwork. We'll offer you refreshments while you relax in our comfortable waiting area.

2. Comprehensive Consultation

You'll meet with one of our experienced dentists who will listen to your concerns and goals. We'll discuss your dental history, lifestyle, and what you hope to achieve with your smile.

3. Thorough Examination

We'll perform a complete oral examination, including digital X-rays and intraoral photography if needed. This helps us understand your current oral health and identify any issues that need attention.

4. Treatment Planning

Based on your goals and our findings, we'll create a customized treatment plan. We'll explain all available options, show you potential results using digital smile design, and provide transparent pricing information.

5. No Pressure Decision Making

There's no obligation to proceed with treatment immediately. We encourage you to take time to consider your options. We'll provide you with all the information you need to make an informed decision about your dental care.

Preparing for Your Visit

  • Bring your medical aid card and any recent dental records or X-rays
  • Arrive 10 minutes early to complete paperwork
  • Make a list of questions or concerns you'd like to discuss
  • If you have dental anxiety, let us know so we can make special arrangements

Transform Your Smile with Expert Care

At Cosmetic Dental Cape Town, we offer a comprehensive range of advanced dental services designed to enhance both the health and beauty of your smile. Our team of specialists uses the latest technology and techniques to deliver exceptional results that exceed your expectations. Whether you're looking for a subtle enhancement or a complete smile transformation, we have the expertise and experience to help you achieve your goals.

Professional Teeth Whitening

Brighten your smile dramatically with our professional teeth whitening treatments. Unlike over-the-counter products, our medical-grade whitening systems can safely lighten your teeth by up to 8 shades in just one visit, giving you the brilliant, confident smile you've always wanted.

Our Whitening Options:

  • In-Office ZOOM! Whitening: Get dramatic results in just 90 minutes with our advanced LED-activated whitening system
  • Custom Take-Home Trays: Professional-grade whitening in the comfort of your home with custom-fitted trays
  • Combination Treatment: Maximize results with both in-office and take-home treatments

Benefits of Professional Whitening:

  • Safe and controlled treatment supervised by dental professionals
  • Dramatic results that last 1-3 years with proper care
  • Minimal to no sensitivity with our advanced formulas
  • Even whitening without spots or streaks
  • Customized treatment based on your specific needs

Professional teeth whitening is ideal for removing stains caused by coffee, tea, red wine, tobacco, and natural aging. During your consultation, we'll assess your teeth and recommend the best whitening option for your lifestyle and desired results.

Porcelain Veneers

Transform your smile completely with custom-crafted porcelain veneers. These ultra-thin shells of medical-grade ceramic are bonded to the front of your teeth, creating a flawless, natural-looking smile that can last 15-20 years with proper care.

What Veneers Can Correct:

  • Discolored or stained teeth that don't respond to whitening
  • Chipped, cracked, or worn teeth
  • Gaps and spaces between teeth
  • Misaligned or uneven teeth
  • Teeth that are too small or misshapen

The Veneer Process:

Our veneer treatment typically requires just 2-3 visits. We begin with a comprehensive consultation where we use digital smile design technology to show you exactly how your new smile will look. Once you approve the design, we prepare your teeth with minimal alteration and take precise impressions. Your custom veneers are crafted by master ceramists to match your desired shade and shape perfectly. Finally, we bond the veneers to your teeth with precision, creating your dream smile.

Why Choose Our Veneers:

  • Natural-looking results that mimic real tooth enamel
  • Stain-resistant material that maintains its beauty
  • Minimal tooth preparation preserves natural structure
  • Digital smile design for predictable results
  • Long-lasting solution with proper care

Dental Implants

Replace missing teeth permanently with dental implants ‚Äì the gold standard in tooth replacement. Our implants look, feel, and function just like natural teeth, allowing you to eat, speak, and smile with complete confidence.

Types of Implant Solutions:

  • Single Tooth Implants: Replace individual missing teeth without affecting adjacent healthy teeth
  • Implant Bridges: Replace multiple missing teeth with implant-supported bridges
  • All-on-4/All-on-6: Full arch restoration with just 4-6 strategically placed implants
  • Implant Dentures: Secure, stable dentures that snap onto implants

The Implant Advantage:

  • Preserve jawbone and prevent bone loss
  • No damage to adjacent healthy teeth
  • Permanent solution that can last a lifetime
  • Natural appearance and function
  • Improved speech and eating ability
  • Enhanced facial structure and appearance

Our implant process uses advanced 3D imaging and computer-guided surgery for precise placement and optimal results. We offer sedation options to ensure your complete comfort throughout the procedure. With proper care, dental implants have a success rate of over 95% and can last a lifetime.

Invisalign Clear Aligners

Straighten your teeth discreetly with Invisalign ‚Äì the virtually invisible alternative to traditional braces. Using a series of custom-made, clear aligners, Invisalign gradually moves your teeth into perfect position without metal brackets or wires.

Invisalign Can Treat:

  • Crowded or crooked teeth
  • Gaps and spaces between teeth
  • Overbite, underbite, and crossbite
  • General teeth alignment issues

Benefits of Invisalign:

  • Nearly invisible ‚Äì most people won't know you're wearing them
  • Removable for eating, drinking, and special occasions
  • No food restrictions
  • Easier oral hygiene than traditional braces
  • Comfortable with no metal irritation
  • Fewer office visits required
  • Predictable results with 3D treatment planning

Treatment typically takes 12-18 months, depending on your case complexity. We use advanced iTero digital scanning to create precise 3D images of your teeth, eliminating the need for messy impressions. You'll see a virtual representation of your treatment plan and final results before you even begin.

Complete Smile Makeovers

A smile makeover is a comprehensive treatment plan that combines multiple cosmetic procedures to completely transform your smile. We customize each makeover to address your specific concerns and achieve your aesthetic goals.

Makeover Treatments May Include:

  • Porcelain veneers or crowns
  • Professional teeth whitening
  • Dental implants or bridges
  • Invisalign or orthodontic treatment
  • Gum contouring and reshaping
  • Composite bonding
  • Full mouth reconstruction

The Smile Makeover Process:

Your journey begins with a comprehensive consultation where we discuss your goals, concerns, and expectations. We perform a thorough examination including digital X-rays, photographs, and 3D scans. Using digital smile design software, we create a customized treatment plan and show you a preview of your new smile. We then coordinate your treatments in the most efficient sequence, often completing multiple procedures in single visits when possible.

Benefits of a Smile Makeover:

  • Comprehensive solution addressing all aesthetic concerns
  • Coordinated treatment for optimal results
  • Improved oral health and function
  • Boosted confidence and self-esteem
  • Long-lasting, life-changing results

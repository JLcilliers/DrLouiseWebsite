About Us

Welcome to Cosmetic Dental, Cape Town's premier clinic for advanced digital and cosmetic dentistry, offering breathtaking views of Table Mountain. Led by principal dentist and director Dr. Louis Trichardt, our dynamic team delivers patient centered care, creating healthy, beautiful smiles tailored to your needs, from subtle enhancements to complete transformations.

We excel in smile transformations and full mouth rehabilitations using cutting edge tools like the 3Shape Trios 5 intraoral scanner and Vatech Smart X CBCT for precise digital protocols. All designs and manufacturing are handled by BOSH Laboratories, specialists in advanced digital technologies. Emphasizing minimally invasive dentistry, we preserve natural tooth structure through treatments like clear aligners, injection-moulded composite veneers, and biofilm removal with the Woodpecker PT-B.

Beyond cosmetics, we provide comprehensive general dentistry, routine check-ups, and preventive hygiene services to maintain your oral wellness, including professional cleanings and early issue detection.

Our focus is on you: We listen, educate, and craft personalized plans fitting your goals, lifestyle, and budget. Contact us today to schedule a consultation with Dr. Trichardt and start your journey to a confident smile!

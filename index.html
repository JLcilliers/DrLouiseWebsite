Welcome to Cosmetic Dental Cape Town

Located in the prestigious Claremont area of Cape Town, Cosmetic Dental has been the premier destination for smile transformations since 2004. Our state-of-the-art facility combines cutting-edge dental technology with a warm, welcoming atmosphere to provide you with an exceptional dental experience that exceeds expectations.

We specialize in comprehensive cosmetic and restorative dentistry, offering everything from professional teeth whitening and porcelain veneers to advanced dental implants and full smile makeovers. Our team of internationally trained dentists and specialists are passionate about creating beautiful, healthy smiles that boost your confidence and improve your quality of life.

What sets us apart is our commitment to personalized care. We understand that every patient is unique, which is why we take time to listen to your concerns and goals before creating a customized treatment plan that fits your needs, timeline, and budget. Using advanced 3D imaging and digital smile design technology, we can show you exactly what your new smile will look like before we begin any treatment.

Our practice features the latest in dental technology, including same-day CEREC crowns, laser dentistry for painless procedures, and 3D cone beam CT scanning for precise implant placement. We also offer sedation options for anxious patients, ensuring your complete comfort throughout your treatment.

Ready to discover what Cosmetic Dental can do for your smile? Schedule your complimentary consultation today and take the first step toward the confident, radiant smile you deserve. New patients receive a free digital smile design preview!

Frequently Asked Questions

Do you offer payment plans?

Yes! We offer 0% interest payment plans for up to 12 months, as well as extended finance options through our medical finance partners.

Is the consultation really free?

Absolutely! New patients receive a complimentary consultation including digital X-rays and a personalized treatment plan worth R1,500.

How long do veneers last?

With proper care, porcelain veneers typically last 15-20 years. We also offer a 10-year warranty on all our veneer treatments.

Do you accept medical aid?

Yes, we work with all major medical aids and can submit claims directly on your behalf for covered procedures.

Excellence Through Expertise

At Cosmetic Dental Cape Town, our team of highly qualified professionals brings together decades of combined experience in cosmetic and restorative dentistry. Each member of our team is passionate about creating beautiful, healthy smiles and committed to providing exceptional patient care. We continuously invest in advanced training and the latest dental technologies to ensure you receive the best possible treatment outcomes.

Dr. Louis Trichardt

Principal Dentist & Clinical Director

BDS (Wits), PGDip Aesthetic Dentistry (UCT)

With over 20 years of experience in cosmetic dentistry, Dr. Anderson is recognized as one of Cape Town's leading smile transformation specialists. After graduating with distinction from the University of Witwatersrand, he completed advanced training in aesthetic dentistry at UCT and has since transformed thousands of smiles.

Dr. Anderson is passionate about combining artistry with cutting-edge dental technology to create natural-looking, beautiful results. He regularly attends international conferences and training programs to stay at the forefront of cosmetic dentistry innovations. His gentle approach and attention to detail have earned him a reputation for excellence among patients and peers alike.

Specialties:

  • Complete smile makeovers
  • Porcelain veneers
  • Complex aesthetic cases
  • Digital smile design

Kayleigh Cheketri

Specialist Prosthodontist

BChD (Stellenbosch), MChD Prosthodontics (UWC)

Dr. Chen is a specialist prosthodontist with expertise in complex restorative and cosmetic cases. She completed her undergraduate training at Stellenbosch University before specializing in prosthodontics at the University of the Western Cape, where she graduated cum laude.

Her meticulous approach to treatment planning and execution ensures predictable, long-lasting results. Dr. Chen has published several papers on aesthetic dentistry and is frequently invited to speak at dental conferences. She is particularly skilled in full mouth rehabilitations and implant restorations, helping patients with severely compromised dentitions regain both function and aesthetics.

Specialties:

  • Dental implants
  • Full mouth reconstruction
  • Complex crown and bridge work
  • TMJ disorders

Wilmarie Kriel

Orthodontist

BDS (UCT), MDent Orthodontics (Wits)

Dr. Mitchell brings over 15 years of orthodontic expertise to our team. After completing his dental degree at UCT, he specialized in orthodontics at the University of Witwatersrand. He is certified in multiple clear aligner systems including Invisalign, where he holds Diamond Provider status.

Known for his innovative treatment approaches, Dr. Mitchell combines traditional orthodontic principles with modern technology to achieve optimal results in the shortest possible time. He has successfully treated over 2,000 Invisalign cases and is passionate about creating beautiful smiles through minimally invasive techniques.

Specialties:

  • Invisalign (Diamond Provider)
  • Accelerated orthodontics
  • Adult orthodontics
  • Pre-restorative orthodontics

Buhle December

Cosmetic Dentist

BChD (UP), PGDip Cosmetic Dentistry (UCT)

Dr. van der Merwe is passionate about conservative cosmetic dentistry and helping patients achieve their dream smiles. She graduated from the University of Pretoria before completing her postgraduate diploma in cosmetic dentistry at UCT, where she received the award for clinical excellence.

With a keen eye for aesthetics and a gentle touch, she specializes in smile design and minimally invasive cosmetic procedures. Dr. van der Merwe stays current with the latest techniques through continuous education and is particularly skilled in composite bonding artistry and teeth whitening procedures.

Specialties:

  • Composite bonding
  • Teeth whitening
  • Smile design
  • Minimally invasive dentistry

Angela Roberts

Our Team Philosophy

At Cosmetic Dental Cape Town, we believe that exceptional dental care is delivered through collaboration, continuous learning, and genuine care for our patients' wellbeing. Our team meets regularly to discuss cases, share knowledge, and ensure that every patient benefits from our collective expertise.

We invest heavily in ongoing education, with team members regularly attending local and international conferences, workshops, and training programs. This commitment to professional development ensures that we remain at the forefront of cosmetic dentistry, offering our patients the latest and most effective treatments available.

Most importantly, we understand that visiting the dentist can be stressful for many people. That's why we've created a warm, welcoming environment where patients feel valued, heard, and cared for. Every member of our team is dedicated to making your experience as comfortable and positive as possible.
